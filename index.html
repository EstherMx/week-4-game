Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the begining of the game

There are four Crystal below. By clicking on a crystal you'll add a specific amount to your total score

You win the game by matching your guessScore score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal
